🧠 AMR Awareness

🦠 What is AMR?

Antimicrobial resistance occurs when bacteria stop responding to medicines designed to kill them.

💊 Responsible Antibiotic Use

Using antibiotics only when needed helps slow down resistance.

🛡️ Prevention

Good hygiene, vaccination and proper treatment help reduce AMR spread.

🔮 Future of AMR Monitoring

Future healthcare systems will use Artificial Intelligence, real-time data analysis and advanced research to detect antimicrobial resistance faster and support better treatment decisions.
