About the India AMR Map

The India AMR Risk Map provides a visual overview of antimicrobial resistance reported across Indian states and union territories. The dashboard combines hospital surveillance, AI-based risk prediction, and trend analysis to help healthcare professionals identify high-risk regions quickly.
