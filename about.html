🦠 About Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)

Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) is one of the greatest global health challenges. It occurs when microorganisms become resistant to medicines, making infections harder to treat and increasing the risk of disease spread.

🦠 What is AMR?

AMR happens when bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites no longer respond to medicines that once treated them effectively.

⚠️ Causes

Overuse and misuse of antibiotics, poor infection control, self-medication, and excessive antibiotic use in animals.

🌍 Global Impact

AMR increases healthcare costs, prolongs illness, and threatens modern medical treatments across the world.

🛡️ Prevention

Use antibiotics responsibly, improve hygiene, promote vaccination, and strengthen infection prevention measures.

🎯 Why AMR Sentinel?

AMR Sentinel helps monitor resistance trends, analyze risks, and support better public health decisions using data and AI.
